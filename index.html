How to Setup & Play

1. Keyboard Fallback (Quick Play)

No webcam? No problem! Use standard keyboard controls:

  • Left / Right Arrow (or A / D): Move Spaceship
  • Spacebar: Shoot Lasers
  • S Key: Activate Deflector Shield
  • P Key: Pause Mission

2. Neural Gesture Controls

This mission uses a preloaded Teachable Machine model trained with hand positions:

  • Left: Tilt or move your hand to the left to steer left.
  • Right: Tilt or move your hand to the right to steer right.
  • Middle: Keep your hand in the center to maintain course.

Simply click ENABLE WEBCAM on the panel to turn on gesture tracking.

3. Game Mechanics

  • Shoot meteors to gain points. Larger meteors split into smaller, faster ones.
  • Dodge meteors to protect your hull integrity.
  • Collect powerups: Shield (invulnerability), Double Shot (extra damage), and Repairs (+25% hull).
  • Deflector Shield drains over time but charges back up slowly.
